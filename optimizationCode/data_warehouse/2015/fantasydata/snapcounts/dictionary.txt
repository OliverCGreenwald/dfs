Snap Count Dictionary

Snaps  total offensive or defensive snaps played by player
Snaps/Gm  Average number of offensive or defensive snaps played per game
Snap Pct  Percentage of offensive or defensive snaps played (only includes games where player played)
Rush Pct  Percentage of snaps played where player had a rushing attempt
Tgt Pct  Percentage of snaps played where player was targeted by a pass attempt
Touch Pct  Percentage of snaps played where player touched the football (includes pass attempts, rush attempts and receptions)
Util Pct  Percentage of snaps played where player touched the football or was targeted (includes pass attempts, rush attempts and receiving targets)
Fantasy Points  Fantasy points scored by player
Points/100 Snaps  Average fantasy points scored by player per 100 snaps
